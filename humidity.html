Humidity

A city's latitude and recorded humidity levels do not show an obvious correlation. Humidity is a measure of the amount of moisture in the air while relative humidity is the amount of moisture in the air compared to the amount of moisture the air can actualy hold. Areas with higher levels of humidity are typically warmer and near large bodies of water.
